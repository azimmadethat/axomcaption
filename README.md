# AxomCaption 🎬

**The first AI subtitle tool built for the Assamese language.**

Generate accurate অসমীয়া subtitles for your videos in seconds. Built for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and any short-form content creators in Assam.

🌐 **Live at:** [axomcaption.in](https://axomcaption.in)

---

## What it does

- Drop a video (up to 1 minute) → click Generate → get Assamese subtitles instantly
- AI transcribes speech directly into অসমীয়া script
- English loanwords stay in English (mobile, video, Instagram, etc.)
- 10 caption styles built for Instagram Reels (Hormozi, Neon, Flash, and more)
- Customize font, color, position, animation, size
- Export as SRT, VTT, or burned-in 1080p video
- 100% runs in the browser — your video never leaves your device

## How to use

1. Go to [axomcaption.in](https://axomcaption.in)
2. Drop your video or click Browse
3. Click Generate
4. Customize your caption style
5. Export

No login. No signup. No upload to any server. Free.

## Tech stack

- Single HTML file — no framework, no build step
- Google Gemini AI (File API) for transcription
- Vanilla JS, CSS
- Hosted on Vercel

## Local development

Just open `index.html` in your browser. That's it.
